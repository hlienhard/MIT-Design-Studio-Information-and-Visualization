Phone Pole

This shows three aspects of the weather: the precipitation, if any, temperature, and humidity. Precipitation changes in the background based on hourly data, the sky will also become dark between 6pm and 6am. Humidity is shown through the position of the bird on the wire - as humidity increases it moves closer to the right side of the screen.


Flower

This shows the temperature and probability of precipitation, as well as the kind of precipitation (again through the background). At the top, the flower changes it's reaction to the weather based on precipitation, wind, and temperature.
